Good to know

Frequently asked

Is the pricing in the configurator final?

It's a live estimate to help you design within budget. Once you send a quote request we confirm the exact price including finishes, delivery and any installation.

Can I fix it to the wall or is it free-standing?

Both. The poles can run floor-to-ceiling for a free-standing look, or be wall-anchored for extra rigidity on taller configurations.

Can I change the layout later?

Yes — that's the point. Planks clamp to the poles, so you can move shelves, add bays or swap a plank whenever you like.

What sizes are possible?

Anything within the ranges in the configurator, set to the millimetre. Need something larger or a special material? Just mention it in your quote request.

Do you deliver and install?

Delivery is available, and installation is offered as an option — let us know your location when you request a quote.

Ready to build yours?

Design it exactly how you want it, then send it over for a quote. No commitment — just your perfect wall.
